What is Matrix Inu?

Regardless of what the name implies, Meme capital is not a venture fund or capital fund or any of that. Matrix Inu is a community driven project on the Ethereum network with unique and interesting tokenomics, reflective mechanism, buyback integration and marketing incorporation. Besides it’s catchy name and interesting tokenomics, Matrix Inu stands out from other Capital projects with its incorporated buyback integration. Tokenomics

Tokenomics

On buys:

A 10% tax on buys is incorporated into the token mechanics which is evenly distributed to all Matrix Inu holders according to their holdings earning every holder passive yield on their holdings.

On sells:

A 10% sell tax is equally incorporated into the Matrix Inu tokenomics of which 5% is auto converted to $ETH and deposited into the farming + buyback wallet. The other 5% is also auto converted to $ETH and deposited into the marketing wallet making Matrix Inu sustainable in the long run.

Farming and Compounded Buybacks

The farming and buyback incorporation is the core and most critical of Matrix Inu token functionality.


5% sell tax is incorporated into the tokenomics which auto converts into ETH at the point of sale and deposits into the buyback wallet which is then bridged and positioned strategically into other EVM compatible chains - Fantom, Avalanche, Polygon, BS, etc…. Daily Buyback Incorporation:- This is initiated randomly every 24hrs to prevent front running bots. Matrix Inu tokens are bought from the market and paired with $ETH, deposited in the liquidity pool and sent to the burn address thus increasing the price floor of each token and making Matrix Inu more liquid.

Conclusion

Matrix Inu tokenomics is setup in a way that the tokens go UpOnly plus holders are eligible to earn both passive yield from reflection and massive yield across the metaverse by holding $MC which is also backed by daily compounded buybacks and Meme Power.
