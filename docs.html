Introduction to FantomChad Capital

What is FantomChad Capital?

Regardless of what the name implies, FantomChad Capital is not a venture fund or capital fund or any of that. FantomChad Capital is a community driven project on the Fantom network with unique and interesting tokenomics, auto-lp generation, buyback integration and marketing incorporation.Besides it’s catchy name and interesting tokenomics, FantomChad Capital stands out from other Capital projects with its incorporated buyback integration.

Token Function

⁃ Buys & Sells: A 15% buy and sell tax is equally incorporated into the FantomChad Capital tokenomics of which 5% is auto converted to $FTM and deposited into the Chad vault (farming + buyback wallet), 5% is deposited into liquidity via the auto-LP generation making the price floor ever increasing and making $FCC insusceptible to price swings. The remaining 5% is auto converted to $FTM and deposited into the marketing wallet making FantomChad Capital sustainable in the long run.

Farming and Synthesized Buybacks.

The farming and buyback incorporation is the core and most critical of FantomChad Capital token functionality. A 5% sell tax is incorporated into the tokenomics which auto converts into FTM at the point of sale and deposits into the Chad Vault which is then bridged and positioned strategically into other EVM compatible chains - Ethereum, Avalanche, Polygon, BSC..etc..

Daily Buyback Incorporation

This is initiated randomly every 24hrs to prevent front running bots. FantomChad Capital tokens are bought from the market and paired with $FTM, deposited in the liquidity pool and sent to the burn address thus increasing the price floor of each token and making FantomChad Capital more liquid.


Marketing Fund

A 5% transaction tax which is auto-converted to $FTM is sent to a marketing wallet and strategically used to organize marketing campaigns , incentivize the community amongst other things, making FantomChad Capital more sustainable in the long run.

Conclusion

FantomChad Capital tokenomics is setup in a way that the tokens go UpOnly plus holders are eligible to earn both passive yield from reflection and massive yield across the metaverse by holding $FCC which is also backed by daily compounded buybacks and Meme Power.
